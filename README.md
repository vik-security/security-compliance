### Security Compliance 

This section outlines widely adopted cybersecurity and compliance frameworks used by organizations to evaluate and strengthen their security posture. Over the years I've worked across a range of cyber security, frameworks and compliance programs. One thing that came up consistently is how confusing and fragmented that information around them can be. For each framework, I've put together a clear overview of what it is, what it's trying to accomplish and how it fits into the bigger picture. The goal is to document security concepts in a clear and practical way, while connecting them to real-world security operations. Once you see those connections, the whole landscape becomes a lot less overwhelming.
